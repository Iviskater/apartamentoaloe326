🌅 Explore Playa del Inglés & Maspalomas Local Guide

  • 🏜️
    Maspalomas Dunes Must-see
    A vast protected nature reserve of golden sand dunes meeting the ocean — like a desert by the sea. Best at sunrise or late afternoon for cooler light and fewer crowds. Stay on the marked paths.
  • 🗼
    Maspalomas Lighthouse (El Faro)
    The historic landmark at the south tip. Stroll the seafront promenade from here past Meloneras — gardens, boutiques and waterfront cafés.
  • 🏖️
    Playa del Inglés Beach
    3 km of golden sand right on your doorstep, flowing into Maspalomas beach. Family zones, surf spots and quieter stretches. Swim between the lifeguard flags.
  • 🦩
    La Charca Pond
    A peaceful bird sanctuary between the dunes and the sea — lovely at sunset.
  • 🦜
    Palmitos Park Family
    Wildlife park in a palm-filled valley — birds of prey, dolphins, parrots. A great family day out (bus line 45). Pre-book tickets.
  • 🛍️
    Yumbo Centrum
    The lively heart of evening Playa del Inglés — shops, restaurants, bars and famous nightly shows. Busiest after dark.
  • 🐫
    Camel Safari & Boat Trips
    Camel rides across the dunes, or catamaran/snorkelling tours along the south coast — easy to book locally.

🚌 Getting Around Local Guide

  • 🚶
    On foot / by bike — The town centre, beach promenade and Yumbo are compact and easily walkable. Loveliest early morning or evening.
  • 🚌
    Buses (Guaguas — Global) — Cheap, clean and reliable across the island. Two stations: Playa del Inglés (Tropical centre, north to Las Palmas) and Maspalomas (by the lighthouse, south coast resorts).
    Handy lines: 30 → Las Palmas · 32/33 → Puerto de Mogán · 45 → Palmitos Park · 66 → Airport.
  • ✈️
    Airport transfer — Bus 66 or 30 are the fastest (≈ €3.50–4). A taxi is roughly €50–60 door-to-door.
  • 🚕
    Taxis — Official taxis are metered; ranks at the bus stations and resort centre. Best for late nights or heavy luggage.
  • 🚗
    Car hire — Worth it only for inland day trips (mountain villages, Fataga, Tejeda). Otherwise buses cover the coast well.

🗺️ Worthwhile Day Trips Local Guide

⛵ Puerto de Mogán

The pretty "little Venice" harbour town — canals, seafood by the water. Bus 32/33.

🏙️ Las Palmas

The capital: historic Vegueta old town & beautiful Las Canteras beach. Bus 30.

⛰️ Fataga & the Mountains

Spectacular drive into pretty hill villages (bus 18) — a different side of the island.

🔭 Mirador Astronómico

Degollada de las Yeguas viewpoint — superb stargazing with little light pollution.
