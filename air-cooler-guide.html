How to Use the Air Cooler

A quick, simple guide for your stay at Apartamento Aloe

MIDEA · AIR COOLER · MCF23XRB

i Good to know first

This is an evaporative air cooler — it cools the air using water (and optional ice), not like a traditional A/C. For the best cool breeze, add water to the tank before turning it on. It works best with a window slightly open for fresh airflow.

1 Quick start (3 steps)

  1. Fill the water tank. Slide out the tank at the bottom, fill with cool water between the MIN and MAX lines, then push it back until it clicks.
  2. Press Power ⏻ to turn it on.
  3. Press the Cool/Humidify button ≋ to start the cool, moist breeze. That’s it — enjoy!
💡 Want it extra cold? Freeze the ice box (see step 4) and drop it in the water tank.

2 The control panel (on top of the unit)

Tap the buttons on the top surface. The large display shows the speed or timer.

⏻
Power
Turns the unit on or off.
≋
Cool / Humidify
Turns the water-cooled (humidifying) breeze on or off. Leave it off for a plain fan breeze.
↔️
Swing (OSC)
Makes the unit swing side to side to spread air across the room.
🌀
Speed
Changes how strong the breeze is. Tap to cycle through the levels.
Ⓜ️
Mode
Switches between the wind modes (see below). Tap to cycle through them.
🕐
Timer
Turns the unit off automatically after a set number of hours. Tap, then keep tapping to choose the hours — the display shows e.g. “12h”.

3 The remote control 🎮

Works from up to 6 metres away. Same functions as the panel:

↔️
Swing (top-left)
Start / stop the side-to-side swing.
⏻
Power (top-right)
Turn on or off.
Ⓜ️
M — Mode (top of circle)
Cycle through the wind modes.
➖
− and + (left / right)
Lower or raise the fan speed.
≋
··· (centre button)
Turn the cool / humidify breeze on or off.
🕐
Clock (bottom of circle)
Set the auto-off timer.

4 Wind modes

Press Mode (M) to switch. Press Speed / + − to fine-tune the strength.

🌬️
Normal
Steady breeze, multiple strength levels
🌡️
Sensi Cool
Auto-adjusts the breeze to the room temperature
🌙
Quiet / Sleep
Soft, gradually gentler — ideal for the night
👶
Baby
Extra-soft, gentle airflow
✨ Negative ions are on by default to help keep the air fresh — nothing to set up.

5 For colder air: the ice box ❄️

  1. Freeze it. Put the ice crystal box in the freezer for at least 3 hours.
  2. Add it to the tank. Slide out the water tank, place the frozen box inside, and make sure water stays below the MAX line.
  3. Turn on “Cooler.” The breeze will feel noticeably colder. The box can be re-frozen and reused. (Regular ice cubes work too!)

! A few friendly reminders

💧
Keep water above the MIN line
🪟
Crack a window for fresh air
🚫
Don’t put objects on top
🧴
Water only — no oils or scents
⚠️ Please don’t tilt or move the unit while it’s full of water, and keep fingers away from the air outlet while it’s running. Thank you for taking care of it! 💙
